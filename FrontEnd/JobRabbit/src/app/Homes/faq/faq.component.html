Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create an account on your website?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the homepage. Fill out the registration form with your personal information and submit it. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

What types of services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services, including but not limited to IT maintenance, cleaning services, vehicle repair, technical support, and more. You can view the full list of available services on our "Services" page.

How do I request a service?

After creating and activating your account, log in and navigate to the "Request a Service" page. Select the desired service, fill in the required details, and submit your request. We will contact you to confirm the details and schedule the service.

How can I track my service request?

You can track the status of your request by logging into your account and accessing the "My Requests" section. Here, you will find a summary of your ongoing and completed requests, along with their status.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment details will be provided once your service request is confirmed.

Can I cancel or modify a service request?

Yes, you can cancel or modify a service request as long as the service has not yet been completed. To do so, log into your account, go to the "My Requests" section, and click on "Modify" or "Cancel" next to the relevant request.

How can I contact customer support if I have an issue?

If you have any questions or concerns, you can reach our customer support team via the contact form on the "Contact Us" page or by phone at the number listed on our website. Our team is available Monday to Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.
